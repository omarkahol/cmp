\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Full covariance bandwidth matrix parameterized by its Cholesky factor. 

Mathematical Formulation Represents a full bandwidth matrix parameterized via lower triangular Cholesky factor $\mathbf{L}$ where\+:   \[\mathbf{H} = \mathbf{L} \mathbf{L}^T
\] The transformation projects distances as\+:   \[\mathbf{z} = \mathbf{L}^{-1} (\mathbf{x}_1 - \mathbf{x}_2)
\] The determinant is computed from the diagonal elements of the Cholesky factor\+:   \[\det(\mathbf{H}) = \prod_{d=1}^D L_{dd}^2
\]
