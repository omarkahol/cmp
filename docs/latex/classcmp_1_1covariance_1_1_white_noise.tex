\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
White noise covariance function. 

Mathematical Formulation Evaluates the Kronecker delta kernel between two inputs $\mathbf{x}_1, \mathbf{x}_2$\+:   \[k(\mathbf{x}_1, \mathbf{x}_2) = \begin{cases} 1.0 & \text{if } d < \text{tol} \\ 0.0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases}
\] where $d$ is the distance between the two points.

Implementation Algorithm Computes distance $d$ and compares it to a small tolerance threshold {\ttfamily tol\+\_\+}.
