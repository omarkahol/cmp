\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Basis\+Type$>$\newline
class cmp\+::\+Quadrature1\+D$<$ Basis\+Type $>$}
One-\/dimensional quadrature rule generator and integrator. 

Mathematical Formulation\+: A 1D quadrature rule approximates the integral of a function \$f(x)\$ over an interval \$\mbox{[}a, b\mbox{]}\$ with a weight function \$w(x)\$ as\+:   \[\int_{a}^{b} w(x) f(x) \, dx \approx \sum_{i=0}^{N-1} w_i f(x_i)
\] where \$x\+\_\+i\$ are the quadrature nodes (roots of the \$\+N\$-\/th order orthogonal polynomial associated with \$w(x)\$) and \$w\+\_\+i\$ are the corresponding quadrature weights. Using the Golub-\/\+Welsch algorithm, the nodes \$x\+\_\+i\$ and weights \$w\+\_\+i\$ are computed from the symmetric tridiagonal Jacobi matrix \$\+J\$ for the orthogonal polynomial sequence\+:         \[J = \begin{pmatrix}
a_0 & b_1 & 0 & \cdots & 0 \\
b_1 & a_1 & b_2 & \cdots & 0 \\
0 & b_2 & a_2 & \ddots & \vdots \\
\vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \ddots & b_{N-1} \\
0 & 0 & \cdots & b_{N-1} & a_{N-1}
\end{pmatrix}
\] The nodes \$x\+\_\+i\$ are the eigenvalues of \$\+J\$. The weights \$w\+\_\+i\$ are computed as\+:   \[w_i = \mu_0 v_{i,0}^2
\] where \$v\+\_\+\{i,0\}\$ is the first component of the normalized \$i\$-\/th eigenvector of \$\+J\$, and \$\textbackslash{}mu\+\_\+0 = \textbackslash{}int\+\_\+\{a\}\texorpdfstring{$^\wedge$}{\string^}\{b\} w(x) \textbackslash{}, dx\$ is the total mass/zeroth moment of the weight function.

Implementation Algorithm\+:
\begin{DoxyEnumerate}
\item Checks if the requested number of points \$\+N\$ is less than 1 (throwing std\+::invalid\+\_\+argument).
\item If \$N == 1\$, sets node \$x\+\_\+0 = 0.\+0\$ and weight \$w\+\_\+0 = 1.\+0\$.
\item Otherwise, requests the Jacobi matrix \$\+J\$ of size \$N \textbackslash{}times N\$ from {\ttfamily Basis\+Type\+::get\+Jacobi\+Matrix(num\+Points)}.
\item Computes the spectral decomposition of \$\+J\$ using {\ttfamily Eigen\+::\+Self\+Adjoint\+Eigen\+Solver}.
\item Assigns the resulting eigenvalues directly to {\ttfamily nodes}.
\item Computes the weights {\ttfamily \doxylink{classcmp_1_1_quadrature1_d_af795366d89e6f9c72d97393ff0e2adf0}{weights(i)}} by squaring the first component of the \$i\$-\/th eigenvector ({\ttfamily solver.\+eigenvectors()(0, i)}).
\item Provides {\ttfamily integrate(f)} which evaluates \$\textbackslash{}sum\+\_\+i w\+\_\+i f(x\+\_\+i)\$. 
\end{DoxyEnumerate}
