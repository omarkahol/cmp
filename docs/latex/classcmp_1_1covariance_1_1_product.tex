\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents the product of two covariance functions. 

Mathematical Formulation For two covariance kernels $k_1$ and $k_2$, the product kernel is\+:   \[k_{\text{prod}}(\mathbf{x}_1, \mathbf{x}_2; \boldsymbol{\theta}) = k_1(\mathbf{x}_1, \mathbf{x}_2; \boldsymbol{\theta}) \times k_2(\mathbf{x}_1, \mathbf{x}_2; \boldsymbol{\theta})
\]
